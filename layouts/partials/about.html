ABOUT US

Eliminating guesswork with the latest guidance technology.

Dedicated to efficiently completing your project on time.

Krahn Const. started as a home building crew in 1987 in Aylmer, ON by George Krahn. He bought his first backhoe in 1995 to help with the construction of the homes. The company moved from there to the Leamington area and it took off into smaller residential excavating jobs, septic systems and such like. KC now takes on larger earthmoving projects, greenhouse site prep, storm drainage with its expanding fleet of equipment and also has the resources and the experience for the smaller scale jobs as well.
